Q.1 Problem: You are given a string (STR) of length N, consisting of only the lower case English alphabet. Your task is to remove all the duplicate occurrences of characters in the string.
Input: abcadeecfb 

Ans:
    let s1= "abcadeecfb";
    let arr1=s1.split("");
    let arr2=new Set(arr1);
    let ans=[...arr2];
    let s2=ans.join("");
    console.log(s2);

output: we got our new string that removal of duplicates.
